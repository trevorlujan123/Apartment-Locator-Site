Renting Problems

Renting problems are no problem for me!

Broken Lease

Many apartment complexes will not work with people who have broken leases in their renting history. If you have a broken lease in your history finding an apartment can be difficult, but with the assistance of an experienced apartment finder it is not!

I will take the stress out of your apartment search and find a complex that will work with you, not against you. Availability will depend on the number of broken leases, area of San Antonio you want to be in, and the amount of money you want to spend.

Evictions

Being evicted from one place can be hard and I understand that sometimes people get caught up in situations that are beyond their control. I will gladly help and put you in touch with complexes that will work with you.

Bad Credit

I understand that bad credit can happen to anyone, even people who do the right thing and even make good money. That is why I have a list of apartment complexes who work with people who have a less than desired credit.
